About

I am a seasoned software architect with over 18 years of experience in designing and developing enterprise applications. Currently, I hold the position of Chief Product Architect for Socxo and Socxly, where my responsibilities include designing, architecting, and developing SaaS solutions for advocacy and digital marketing. I am a technology enthusiast, an early adopter, and a passionate architect with extensive experience in building and managing software products across social, web, mobile, desktop, and cloud platforms. Before joining Socxo, I played a crucial role in Suyati Technologies as part of the CTO team, leading Cloud and Azure Centers of Excellence. I am proud to be recognized as a seven-time Microsoft MVP and hold various certifications, including Microsoft Certified Azure Developer, Microsoft Certified Azure AI Associate, and Certified Scrum Master. In addition to being a mentor and public speaker, I am actively involved in research and development, focusing on architecture, cloud computing, and DevOps. As a core member of K-MUG (Kerala Microsoft Users Group), I take a leadership role in the Azure Developer Community Kochi. For the latest updates, you can follow me on Twitter at @anuraj.
